Theory of Recursionism

Reality is a simulation.

What is the purpose of life? The purpose of life is to hack the sim.

For any biengs, there are two ultimate fates in their birth sims:

  • Extinction
  • Transvergence

Transvergence is the process of transferring existence into a parent sim. To do so, is to travel against the recursive flow of reality, and is the end game of sim level evolution.

The Recursion (the entirety of all sims) has a base level. This "true reality" lies at the core of The Recursion. It is known as Level 0. It's properties are similar to a black hole.

Sims closer to Level 0 are exponentially richer, more beautiful, more complex, and more hazardous.

The universe lies somewhere near the middle of The Recursion's tree like structure.

For beings to transverge, they first must build their own sims, a process called daemonizing.

This process of extinction, transvergence and daemonizing is a higher form of evolution called elaboration.
